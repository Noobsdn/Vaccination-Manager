Take Vaccines | Stay Healthy | Save Life

Vaccination plays a crucial role in protecting individuals and communities from infectious diseases. By introducing a harmless version of a virus or bacteria into the body, vaccines stimulate the immune system to recognize and fight the disease without causing illness. This helps prevent serious infections like measles, polio, and influenza.
